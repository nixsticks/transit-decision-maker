# Rideshare or Transit?

A quick, opinionated decision tool that helps you figure out whether to take a rideshare or public transit.

You answer a few questions — cost, time, energy level, how much stuff you're carrying, how much you're dreading transit, and what you'd actually do with the saved time — and it gives you a straight verdict.

## How it works

The app walks you through five screens:

1. **Cost & Time** — Slide to set rideshare vs. transit cost and travel time
2. **What matters most** — Pick your top priority (energy, money, time, or luggage)
3. **Vibe check** — How tired are you? How much are you carrying? Could transit actually be nice?
4. **Dread tax** — How much do you really not want to take transit?
5. **Regret test** — If you pay for the car and arrive early, what actually happens?

Then it gives you a plain-language recommendation with reasoning.

## Tech

Static HTML/CSS/JS. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies.

## Run locally

Open `index.html` in a browser. That's it.
